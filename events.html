WHO'S LYING?

Rules:

  • Each team will be given a role or identity (real or fictional). One participant will secretly be assigned as the Liar/Imposter with a different or no backstory
  • Players take turns asking only one question per turn to any other player. Questions must relate to the given role/identity or scenario
  • Participants must answer immediately and keep their responses consistent throughout the game

WORD WIZARDS' CUP

Round 1: Online Writing

  • Original Content Only – Your story must be your creation. Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification
  • No Malpractice – Any attempt to cheat will lead to removal from the competition
  • Time Limit – You have 60 minutes to complete and submit your story. Once the timer ends, your work will be auto-submitted
  • Single Attempt Only – You can submit only once. Multiple submissions are not allowed
  • Note – The system actively monitors tab switching, fullscreen exits, and paste attempts. Any violation will instantly disqualify your entry

Round 2: Quick Quill

  • Participants will narrate a story live in front of the judges online
  • At random intervals, judges will introduce unexpected twists, settings, or characters
  • The participant must immediately incorporate each twist into their story without breaking flow
  • Each participant will have 5-7 minutes to complete their storytelling
  • The storyteller who adapts best and delivers the most engaging story wins the round
